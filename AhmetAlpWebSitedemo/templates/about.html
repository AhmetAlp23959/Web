About Financial Tracker

Purpose of the Application

Financial Tracker is designed to help individuals manage their personal finances effectively. By providing tools to track transactions, budgets, and spending habits, our application aims to empower users to make informed financial decisions and achieve their financial goals.

General Idea

The application allows users to record income and expenses, categorize transactions, view financial reports, and gain insights into their financial health through interactive charts and summaries. It's a comprehensive solution for budgeting and managing finances with ease and precision.
